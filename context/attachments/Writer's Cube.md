Premise for the product: You think you have writer’s block? Think again\! Now you’ve got writer’s cube \- a new way of writing in a way that fits the way you work. 

This product takes the ideas of google docs, Coda, and Scrivener, and customizes them for creative writers. I don’t want to duplicate any of those three products, I want to take the best from each and create the ideal one-stop-shop for creative writing needs. The goal is to create an environment that inspires and motivates writers to start and finish a creative piece. It should mimic aspects of both the digital and physical writing spaces, allowing the user customization ability to shape their space.
